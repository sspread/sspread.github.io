Directions

  1. Joe the mosquito is hiding in the jungle.
  2. You have 4 grenades to blow him up.
  3. Grenades inflict a max of 10 damage.
  4. Grenades have a 10 unit damage radius.
  5. Damage is reduced by 1 for each unit of distance.
  6. Use damage to locate Joe.
  7. A direct hit is an instant kill!
