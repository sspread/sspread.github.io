Pair Programming

Driver and Navigator

  • Driver is typing code into the editor
  • Navigator is dictating what is typed
  • The person with the idea is the navigator; this can switch fluidly

Why is pair programming important to DBC?

  • When you have two people working on something, you can fill in the gaps in one another's knowledge.
  • Two brains are better than one
  • The driving/navigating structure collectively allows focus on both thought process and syntax.

First Experience...

  • It was strange to pair with someone over the computer.
  • I enjoyed the opportunity to bounce ideas.
  • Pairing with someone helped keep me on track, as I tend to explore things I don't understand.
  • The view feature on Stypi didn't work. My pair was MIA.
